Are you a member of a company or a government person?

You can now choose whether you are a member of a company or a government representative for certain regions. You can also be both at the same time.

If you are a member of a company you can create, edit and store parking data for your company's parkings. This data will be shared amongst other members of your company.

If you apply for regional representative, you will be able to access and approve all the parkingdata for your region.

Both of these privilleges will have to be accepted by our system admins before they can be used.
